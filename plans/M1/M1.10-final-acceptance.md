- **SC1091**: `shellcheck` is unable to statically follow dynamic sourcing paths like `source "$(cd "$(dirname "${BASH_SOURCE[0]}")" && pwd)/_claim-common.sh"`.
- **SC2034**: Variables like `CLAIM_ROOT` and `CLAIM_VALIDATOR` trigger "appears unused" warnings because they are exclusively utilized by the dynamically sourced `_claim-common.sh`.

**Action**: Implement targeted inline suppression in the 12 shims.
- Add `# shellcheck disable=SC2034` directly above the `CLAIM_ROOT` and `CLAIM_VALIDATOR` variable exports.
- Adjust the `# shellcheck source=./_claim-common.sh` directive so it is tightly bound to the `source` command to properly suppress SC1091 (or append `# shellcheck disable=SC1091` to the line).
